Lore

Game Explanation

Xtream chaser is a fighting-themed game that will take you to the dream world. You can find various ways to make you the greatest player.
There are various game modes available, which can be accessed if the player has completed all the main missions in the story mode.
It is important to know that you don't need to be lucky to complete this game, because we all know that skill is the most important thing in the game.

Story Mode

Xtream Chaser story mode consists of dozens of chapters, with a background in imperial times in the East Asia region.
Many characters are ready to welcome you in story mode to adventure and fight with you.

Towers of Times

This game mode is available if you have completed the main story mode mission,
there are various challenges that have varying levels of difficulty and also the requirements for each challenge.
Players can play this game mode to increase the level of character and also get rare gears.

League

Interested in becoming the king of the world? You can challenge other players and improve your skills to be number 1 and invincible.
Choose your favorite character to accompany you to the top of the league.
The top-ranked player every season will be invited to the annual Xtream Chaser championship.
